UNDER CONSTRUCTION

Brutalist web design for the rest of us.

Today's websites are too boring, too unoriginal, and weirdly optimistic. Join the movement of designers that have decided to do the exact opposite on the internet, creating wild and original user-UNfriendly experiences, using harsh typography and colours, just like the good old days of web design.

In the past, creating these sorts of websites was a simple task, but with the standards of web design constantly changing it’s difficult to keep up. What if you could provide all the content you needed to a service and have it make a website for you? Under Construction is your answer.

Use Under Construction to create “unique” looking:

  • Portfolios
  • Event pages
  • Product demonstrations

Type here and give it a whirl for yourself!

Place all of your page content in this box and watch a 1-page website appear in front of your very eyes. Customize the result however you feel like. Change only the elements that you want or just toggle everything and generate a beautiful trashterpiece. When you’re done, save your website and host it on your hosting provider of choice.
